Urea Synthesis Reactor Conversion Calculator (Beta Version)

Description:

The Urea Synthesis Reactor Conversion Calculator is designed to calculate the conversion rate in a urea synthesis reactor.

The calculation is based on the following input parameters:

Molar ratio of ammonia to CO₂ and water to CO₂ in the synthesis melt.

Synthesis pressure in the reactor.

Partial pressure percentage of inert gases in the reactor.

Calculation Results:

CO₂ conversion rate to urea.

Equilibrium temperature, at which the given state is achieved.

Material balance:

Composition of the liquid phase (synthesis melt).

Composition of the gas phase (stream leaving the reactor).

This beta version is intended for end-user testing before final refinement.

For feedback and suggestions, please refer to the contact information.
